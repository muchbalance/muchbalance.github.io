Use :

By entering your dogecoin address in input field, you will get your - dogecoin wallet balance in dogecoin & in USD, a QR Code of your address, received dogecoin amount in dogecoin, spent dogecoin amount in dogecoin.

There is an refresh button just after price shown in USD, by clicking on that button, price of dogecoin in USD will be refreshed.

This website can behave like a mobile app, just add it to your homescreen(Click on 3 dots or on menu in your browser and there will be a option Add To Home Screen), and now you can use it like an app.
